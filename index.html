What does Biztech Labs do?

We help educate students on how technology can be used in many different domains they might be interested in. We increase students awareness with our own educational workshops and student competitions where they will learn how to work in groups to solve a real world problem in areas like health and wellbeing, finance, education and more.

Whether it's coming up with a concept to improve the health and wellbeing sector, looking at a new idea for a financial company, or just coming along to grow their education in technology, we encourage all with an interest in technology to come along to create, innovate and be inspired by the possibilites of this industry.

What happens on the day?

Listen from the industry

Meet with our guest speakers and teachers to learn where a career in technology can lead you.

Learn something new

Learn applicable and practical knowledge that may not always be taught in the classroom.

Meet new friends

Meet some new friends from different schools, get amongst the free food and compete for winning prizes.

Who's this event for?

These events are open for all intermediate and high school students with ages ranging from 11 to 18 years old. No matter your background, gender or culture, all are welcome to come explore the skills you might need to build a solution with technology that can help solve a modern day issue.
